Lasagna

Description

A tasty Italian dish where a meat sauce is layered with noodles and cheese. Simple and easy to do!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and tomato paste
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings

Preparation

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodle plates
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat from the top then finish with the remaining Parmesan
  5. Slap the lasagna in the oven for 50 minutes at 375f/190c
  6. When taking the lasagna out, let it rest at room temp for 15mins before digging in

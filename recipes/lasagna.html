Lasagna

Description

Tasty lasagna

Ingredients

  • minced meat
  • lasagna plates
  • cheese
  • cream
  1. Cook the meat
  2. Boil the plates
  3. Set up plates on the bottom
  4. Put a layer of meat
  5. Put a little cream and cheese
  6. Add another layer of plates
  7. Repeat until out of plates
  8. Add one more layer of meat
  9. Add more cheese and cream
  10. Put the lasagna in the oven

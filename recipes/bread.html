Bread

Description

A simple white bread recipe.

Ingredients

  • Warm water (105-115 deg)
  • Active dry yeast
  • Granulated sugar or honey
  • Salt
  • Oil, vegetable or canola, can also use melted butter
  • Flour, bread or all-purpose

Preparation

  1. The yeast
    1. Add the yeast, water and a pinch of sugar or honey in a large bowl
    2. Let it rest for 5-10 mins until foaming and bubbly. If it doesnt foam, the yeast is bad and you need to start over with fresh yeast
  2. The dough
    1. Add the remaining sugar or honey, salt, oil and 3 cups of flour
    2. Mix them
    3. Add another cup of flour and mix more
    4. Knead the dough for 5-8 minutes
    5. Grease a large bowl with oil or cooking spray and put the dough in. Cover the bowl and let the dough rise in a warm place until double in size
    6. Punch the dough to remove air bubbles
    7. Divide into two equal portions, shape them into long logs and put them into pans
  3. Spray two pieces of plastic wrap with cooking spray and lay them over the pans
  4. Let the doughs to rise again for 45mins-1hr or until risen 1 inch above the pans
  5. Preheat the oven to 350f/176c
  6. Bake the bread for 30-33mins or until golden brown on top. Tap the top of the loaf. It's ready if it sounds hollow
  7. Put the loaves into a cooling rack and brush the tops with butter and allow to cool for 15mins before slicing

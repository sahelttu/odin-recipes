Lohikeitto

Description

Delicious, creamy Finnish salmon soup with veggies for cold fall and winter days. It is super easy and fast to make!

Ingredients

  • Half stick of unsalted butter
  • 1 leek, sliced
  • 1.25l of fish stock
  • 450g of cubed potatoes
  • 1 sliced carrot
  • 450g salmon fillet, de-boned, de-skinned and chopped into chunks
  • 250ml heavy cream
  • 10g fresh dill, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Melt the butter in a pot
  2. Add the leek and saute about 7 mins or until translucent
  3. Add the stock, carrot and potatoes
  4. Bring the soup to a boil and cook for 10mins over medium heat
  5. Add the salmon chunks and the cream and cook for 5-7 more minutes
  6. Add dill, salt and pepper to a taste
  7. Close the lid and wait for another 10 minutes
